I’m a software engineer by day and a part-time creator in the evenings. I spend a lot of hours at this desk coding, editing, and learning, so I put together a setup that stays productive for both.

## The desk

The centerpiece is the [Ergonofis Sway Desk](https://shrsl.com/49346). It has a slick control interface, an anti-collision system, and the kind of build quality that makes standing all day feel natural. They also sent their solid wood monitor riser / desk shelf, which keeps the display at eye level and adds a bit of structure to the surface.

- [Ergonofis Sway Desk](https://shrsl.com/49346): standing desk with intuitive preset heights
- [Ergonofis Desk Shelf](https://shrsl.com/49342): solid wood shelf for posture and organization
- [Ergonofis Cable Management](https://shrsl.com/4933x): keeps the underside tidy

## Monitors and lighting

A good monitor is non-negotiable for my work. I use the LG 34WN780 for its ergonomic stand and wide canvas, plus a BenQ ScreenBar Halo for consistent, glare-free light.

- [LG 34WN780 Monitor](https://amzn.to/3DJd86G)
- [BenQ ScreenBar Halo](https://amzn.to/3fUAfCi)

## Computer setup

My daily driver is a 16-inch MacBook Pro. An Anker USB-C hub cuts down cable clutter, and a Nomad MagSafe dock keeps the phone out of the way.

- [16-inch MacBook Pro](https://amzn.to/41fkhEH)
- [Anker USB-C Hub](https://amzn.to/3Dk9vCV)
- [Nomad MagSafe Dock](https://nomadgoods.com/products/stand-one-silver)

## Audio

I mostly work in headphones because monitor speakers aren’t enough for editing or focus sessions.

- [AirPods Max](https://amzn.to/3mie64b)
- [Apple AirPods Pro](https://amzn.to/3UmMQhq)
- [Sonos Beam Soundbar](https://amzn.to/46h5E6H)

## Peripherals

I’m a mechanical keyboard person. The Mode Envoy with Alexotos linear switches and a GMK Dracula set is still my favorite daily typer. For pointing, I switch between a Logitech MX Master III and an Apple Magic Trackpad.

- [Orbitkey Desk Mat](https://amzn.to/3Dm37eu)
- [Mode Envoy Keyboard](https://modedesigns.com/pages/envoy)
- [Alexotos Linear Switches](https://modedesigns.com/products/alexotos-edition-switches)
- [GMK Dracula Keycap Set](https://geekhack.org/)
- [Logitech MX Master III](https://amzn.to/3U5syHG)
- [Magic Trackpad](https://amzn.to/46gxWyf)

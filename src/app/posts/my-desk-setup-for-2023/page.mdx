## The Desk

First things first: the desk. It's the centerpiece of the setup. I got to test the [Ergonofis Sway Desk](https://shrsl.com/49346), which was gifted to me. It's a game-changer, with a slick control interface and aesthetics that match the functionality. They also built in an anti-collision system.

The Ergonofis team also sent their solid wood monitor riser / desk shelf. Having your monitor at eye level is better for posture, and the shelf is a clean way to get there while adding some style.

- [Ergonofis Sway Desk](https://shrsl.com/49346): A versatile standing desk with intuitive preset heights
- [Ergonofis Desk Shelf](https://shrsl.com/49342): Solid wood shelf to elevate the display
- [Ergonofis Cable Management](https://shrsl.com/4933x): Keeps the underside tidy

## Monitors and Lighting

A high-quality monitor matters for my work, and I chose the LG 34WN780. Its ergonomic stand gives full control over screen positioning. I also added the BenQ ScreenBar Halo, a USB-powered lamp that gives consistent, glare-free light across the desk.

- [LG 34WN780 Monitor](https://amzn.to/3DJd86G): Strong monitor with ergonomic versatility
- [BenQ ScreenBar Halo](https://amzn.to/3fUAfCi): USB-powered lamp for consistent desk illumination

## Computer Setup

My primary machine is the 16-inch MacBook Pro. To streamline connections and cut cable clutter, I use an Anker USB-C hub. For audio, I reach for AirPods Max when I want to focus without distractions.

- [16-inch MacBook Pro](https://amzn.to/41fkhEH): Reliable laptop for day-to-day work
- [Anker USB-C Hub](https://amzn.to/3Dk9vCV): Simplifies connections for a cleaner desk
- [Nomad MagSafe Dock](https://nomadgoods.com/products/stand-one-silver): Premium dock for MagSafe-compatible devices

## Audio

I prefer headphones over the monitor speakers. AirPods Max are the main pair for quality and focus.

- [AirPods Max](https://amzn.to/3mie64b): Primary headphones for deep work
- [Apple AirPods Pro](https://amzn.to/3UmMQhq): Compact earbuds for lighter tasks
- [Sonos Beam Soundbar](https://amzn.to/46h5E6H): Wall-mounted soundbar when I want the room to feel like a mini theater

## Peripherals

I'm a mechanical keyboard enthusiast, and my favorite at the time was the Envoy by Mode Designs. Paired with the GMK Dracula keycap set and a Tomorrow-themed set, it added a personal touch. For pointing, I used the Logitech MX Master III for the extra buttons and ergonomics.

- [Orbitkey Desk Mat](https://amzn.to/3Dm37eu): Desk mat with built-in storage
- [Mode Envoy Keyboard](http://modedesigns.com): Favorite keyboard of that year
- [Alexotos Linear Switches](https://modedesigns.com/products/alexotos-edition-switches): Smooth linear switches; I get a lot of compliments on the sound
- [GMK Dracula Keycap Set](https://geekhack.org/): Custom keycaps with a personal look
- [Logitech MX Master III](https://amzn.to/3U5syHG): Productive, ergonomic mouse
- [Magic Trackpad](https://amzn.to/46gxWyf): Gesture-friendly alternative to a traditional mouse

## Chair and Ergonomics

For long sessions, I chose the Herman Miller Aeron. Fully adjustable, with lumbar support that keeps posture comfortable through the day.

- [Herman Miller Aeron Chair](https://www.hermanmiller.com): Ergonomic chair built for long hours
